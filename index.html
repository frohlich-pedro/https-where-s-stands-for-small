About Me

I'm a 17-year-old developer from Joinville, Santa Catarina, Brazil, with a passion for low-level programming and systems development. My expertise lies in creating efficient, bare-metal solutions using Assembly and C programming languages.


I love pushing the boundaries of what's possible with minimal resources, whether it's fitting a complete Pong game into a 512-byte bootsector or implementing custom memory allocators from scratch. My projects demonstrate a deep understanding of computer architecture and system-level programming.
